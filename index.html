About Thambapanni Trails

Founded in 2015, Thambapanni Trails has been creating unforgettable travel experiences across Sri Lanka. Our mission is to showcase the natural beauty, cultural heritage, and hidden gems of this paradise island while promoting sustainable tourism.

We believe travel should be transformative, authentic, and respectful to the destinations we visit. Every journey with us is carefully crafted to ensure you experience the true essence of Sri Lanka.
